New Year Is For New Beginnings

January 12, 2022
Photo obtained from Anna Tarazevich on Pexels.com

The new year is upon us and it’s really exciting because at this time everyone goes through a little of reflection and write down their goals to achieve during or before the end of 2022. However, only a small percentage of people stick with their goals till the end and the rest seems to focus hard over the first 30 to 60 days before falling back into those old habits. It is therefore very important to know what is your why when we set our goals as they are going to guide us, keep us motivated each day and help us to sustain positive momentum through the ups and downs of life. More importantly, to establish a routine (or process) to support our journey

Over the last couple of years, I have put heaps of efforts into transitioning myself from actuarial/analytics professional into a data/ML engineer. The year 2021 was the cornerstone to this change as I landed my first engineering consulting role with Slalom Build the engineering arm of Slalom the global consulting firm that focuses on strategy, technology, and transformation. Building on the momentum from last year, my main goal this year is to grow my ENGINEERING skills (and everything that it entails). The best way for that is practice, practice and practice.

Here is list of actions, to become routine to me, that I thought of to get me going till the of 2022:

  1. Blogging doing this on a consistent basis will help with generating contents;
  2. Learning I have a couple of certifications to pass this year;
  3. Delivering using my newly acquired knowledge to deliver at work or personal projects.

Lastly, on the 1st February it is Chinese New Year and this year is the year of the Tiger. This is the time to be competitive, courageous and ambitious…so be just that ;)
